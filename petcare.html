Pet Care

  • View on GitHub

Many pet owners enjoy peace of mind knowing that their pet is getting the best possible medical care. COVID-19 has changed ways not only humans, but also pets can receive medical care. With the rise of technology and telehealth, getting medical care has never been easier. Available for both iOS and Android, Pet Care is a mobile application that was developed to allow pet owners to access important information about their pet right in their pockets.

Pet Care is a step toward the future of pet medical care and telehealth for animals. It includes a way for pet owners to save their pets to their profile, view training videos for each of their pets, access their pet’s medical information such as lab results, and diagnose potential diseases that their pet may have by entering symptoms in the app’s diagnostic tool. The application uses React Native to render its UI components in the frontend and Firebase for its login authentication service and to store user, pet, and disease information in the backend.

With its most recent iteration, Pet Care was redesigned with an updated Pet Profile section that allows the user to have more detailed personal information about their pet than ever, such as activity level, size, weight, pregnancy status, etc. Information about the pet’s diet and prescriptions can now be updated and accessed. An application like Pet Care can not only allow pet care providers to make more thoughtful medical decisions but also allow pet owners to have peace of mind knowing that their pet is receiving quality medical care.

Features

Sign-up & Login Authentication

Using Firebase Authentification features users can sign-up using an email and password and login using the set credentials.


Add & Keep Track of Pets

On the pets screen you can add and keep track of your pets and other animals. You can include details such as age, weight, activity level, and pregnancy status. After supplying details on your pet, you can view the details, as well as options to contact their veterinarian, view documents and prescriptions, or view dietary and training suggestions.


Diagnose Your Sick Pets

Using the symptoms stored on the Firestore database, diagnose your sick pets using keywords and symptoms. User will recieve possible diagnoses and information on symptoms and steps to take to help.


View Diet and Training Suggestions

Submit your pets diet and your vet will send diet suggestions for your individual pets. Using the Youtube API the app will also provide you with videos for training your breed of animal.


View Prescriptions and Lab Results

Through the app, veterinarians will be able to provide a user with prescription information and lab result information for their pets.
